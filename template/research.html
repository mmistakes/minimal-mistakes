Funding Opportunities

The Center for Data Intensive Science provides funding and support to University of Chicago researchers for projects leveraging data science with data clouds and data commons. CDIS offers a collaborative approach to stimulate data-driven research across disciplines, defuse technological barriers, and promote discovery through informatics. Pilot projects for the current period have been awarded. Application information for the next award period will be posted in May 2015.
