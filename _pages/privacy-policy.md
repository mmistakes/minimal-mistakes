## Privacy Policy for Aging Health

At **Aging Health**, accessible from [https://aginghealth.website](https://aginghealth.website), one of our primary core values is the absolute privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of aggregate data collected and recorded by our platform and how we manage it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us by email at [tommglo@gmail.com](mailto:tommglo@gmail.com).

### Log Files and General Analytics
Aging Health follows a standard protocol of using log files. These files automatically log visitors when they browse websites. Like almost all standard web hosting environments, this data includes:
* Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
* Browser types and browser versions
* Internet Service Provider (ISP) network signatures
* Date and time stamps of entry/exit
* Referring pages and the number of clicks

This information is utilized solely to analyze high-level performance trends, administer the indexing system, track search performance behavior, and gather broad demographic data. **None of this information is ever linked to personally identifiable data.**

### Cookies and Local Storage Tracking
Our environment uses a tiny piece of local browser memory (Local Storage) to remember your preferences—such as recording whether you have acknowledged our privacy banner so it doesn't continuously interrupt your reading experience. 

We do not deployment tracking cookies or data-mining scripts that follow your identity across external sites.

### Third-Party Data Disclosures
We prioritize open-access distribution and the free sharing of health literacy. **We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any visitor traffic analytics or aggregate data profiles to marketing networks, brokers, or third parties.**
