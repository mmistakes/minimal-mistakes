<section class="resource-section">
	<h2>Mathematical Foundation</h2>
	<p>
		Before delving into Machine Learning and Deep Learning, it's crucial to solidify the mathematical foundations upon
		which these fields are built. Mathematics is the backbone of ML and DL algorithms, and a strong grasp of key concepts
		like probability, statistics, linear algebra, and optimization is essential. This section equips you with those
		foundational skills.
	</p>
	<h3>Resources</h3>
	<p>
		<a href="https://hackmd.io/@eMS9nZq5Tk6-gP09chWyqQ/H1TZcit6a">Prob-Stats 1</a>: Begin with the basics of
		probability and statistics to understand distributions, uncertainty, and decisions.
	</p>
	<p>
		<a href="https://hackmd.io/@ZXxKhF68QyCRN3u4US17ZQ/ryAOaL_0a">Prob-Stats 2</a>: Continue into Bayesian inference
		and hypothesis testing for more complex ML models.
	</p>
	<p>
		<a href="https://hackmd.io/CRuXzA0XRFy5I2Yh51dODg?view">Linear Algebra and Optimisation</a>: Master the operations
		that power neural nets and the optimization methods that tune them.
	</p>
