Welcome to **Aging Health**, an independent archive dedicated to translating complex, modern clinical data into actionable, high-literacy information for seniors, families, and geriatric patient advocates.

### Our Mission: Open-Access Health Literacy
We believe that critical research regarding longevity, metabolic health, and complex pathologies should be free, clear, and unencumbered by corporate paywalls or subscription barriers. This site operates strictly on an open-access model supported entirely by community alignment and individual advocacy efforts.

### Editorial & Verification Standards
* **Evidence-Based Extraction:** Every article published in this catalog is built directly upon peer-reviewed clinical trials, primary metabolic data, and established institutional guidelines (such as the NIH, Mayo Clinic, and the American Heart Association).
* **Zero Commercial Influence:** We do not host sponsored marketing loops, accept pharmaceutical capital, or track visitor data for advertising profiles. 
* **Advocacy Intent:** This resource is designed to empower patients to understand their biomarkers and prepare comprehensive, data-driven frameworks for their next primary care or specialist appointment.
