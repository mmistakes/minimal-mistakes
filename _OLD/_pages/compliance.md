## Common Questions

**Do we really need SOC 2/ISO 27001?**  
If you're selling to enterprises, yes. 95% of Fortune 500 companies require security compliance from vendors. It's not optional anymore‚Äîit's table stakes.

**Can't we just self-attest or fill out questionnaires?**  
Self-attestations don't cut it anymore. Customers want third-party validated reports. One security incident at a vendor costs them millions‚Äîthey won't take your word for it.

**Will compliance slow our development velocity?**  
Done wrong, yes. Done right, it actually accelerates development by reducing security incidents, failed deployments, and customer escalations. We implement compliance as code.

**What if we fail the audit?**  
With proper preparation, you won't. Our 100% pass rate isn't luck‚Äîit's preparation. We know what auditors look for and ensure you're ready before they arrive.

**Can we start with just one framework?**  
Yes, and you should. We typically recommend starting with SOC 2 as it provides the foundation for other frameworks. 70% of SOC 2 controls overlap with ISO 27001 and HIPAA.
