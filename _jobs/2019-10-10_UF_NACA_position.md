This is an open level position for a candidate with a BS/MS or PhD, supported through a collaborative agreement the USDA and  the University of Florida.

# BS/MS level position

For BS/MS candidates the position may be filled under one of several job series including Application programmer, Engineer or Biological Scientist.

## Key Responsibilities:
* Developing and evaluating machine learning models in Tensorflow/Keras in
collaboration with group scientists.
* writing command line python packages and Qiime plugins
* writing web applications using a DevOps approach a typical stack would be (MongoDB/ Eve /Flask/ VueJS)

## Desired Skills:
* Experience in machine learning, statistics, DevOpps practices
computational biology, or computer science
* experience with R, Python
* Strong communication and organizational skills


# Postdoctoral level position

The Postdoctoral position has freedom to develop in collaboration with the PI's
a research plan focusing on one of several areas:

1. The development of methods to link plant or insect host genotypes to their
microbiome through a combination of computational and/or experimental methods.

2. The Development of CRISPR-Cas experimental tools for microbial phenotyping and community manipulation.

3. Experimental validation of methods for microbial consortia engineering

## Desired Skills:

* Experience in machine learning, statistics,
computational biology, or computer science
* Experience with R, Python
* Experience with Microbiology and next generation sequencing

I recognize that each person
has a unique mix of experience and individual strengths and I encourage any
applicant with a strong record of research productivity and relevant experience
to apply, even if your mix of skills is different than what is listed above.
