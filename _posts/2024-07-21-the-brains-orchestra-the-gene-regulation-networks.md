    <h2 class="ah-title">How PsychENCODE Empowers Seniors</h2>
    <p>The <strong>PsychENCODE Breakthrough</strong> is more than just a massive data set; for a senior navigating the complexities of aging, it represents a fundamental shift from a "fixed blueprint" to a <strong>dynamic system</strong> where conductors (regulatory elements) can be influenced.</p>
    
    <h3>1. From "Genetic Destiny" to "Epigenetic Agency"</h3>
    <p>PsychENCODE shows that conditions aren't caused by a single "broken" gene, but by the <strong>timing and volume</strong> of gene expression.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>The Help:</strong> This provides scientific proof that lifestyle interventions (nutrition, sleep, stress management) are effectively "re-training" your brain’s orchestra.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>2. Ending the "Medication Guessing Game"</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>The Help:</strong> Mapping these networks allows for <strong>Precision Pharmacy</strong>. Doctors can eventually target the specific "stuck switch" rather than relying on trial and error.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>3. Early Warning Systems (The "Pre-Symptom" Window)</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>The Help:</strong> With 46,000 regulatory elements identified, we can now develop <strong>biomarker panels</strong> to detect network failure <em>years</em> before physical symptoms manifest.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="ah-card" style="border-top: 4px solid rgb(29, 78, 216);">
    <h2 class="ah-title">Alzheimer’s: Beyond Plaques and Tangles</h2>
    <p>For years, the conversation was stuck on amyloid "gunk." The 2026 view is different: Alzheimer’s isn't just a "clogged pipe" problem; it’s a <strong>"broken software"</strong> problem.</p>
    
    <div class="ah-update-box">
      <strong>1. The "Microglia" Revolution 🛡️</strong><br />
      We are moving away from drugs that just "mop up" plaques and toward <strong>epigenetic therapies</strong> that tell the brain's immune crew (Microglia) to stop attacking and start cleaning.
    </div>

    <div class="ah-update-box">
      <strong>2. APOE4: From "Verdict" to "Variable" 🧬</strong><br />
      Having the gene isn't a destiny. Metabolic health and environment can act as a "mute button" on these high-risk genetic conductors.
    </div>

    <div class="ah-update-box">
      <strong>3. The "Type 3 Diabetes" Connection 🍎</strong><br />
      Controlling blood sugar is now a frontline defense for brain preservation. When the brain can't use glucose, it "starves," leading to decline.
    </div>
