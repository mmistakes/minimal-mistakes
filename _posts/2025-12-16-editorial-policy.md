<p>&nbsp;</p><h2><strong>Editorial Policy</strong></h2><p>At Aging Health, our mission is to provide clear, trustworthy, and accessible health information for older adults, caregivers, and families. We follow strict editorial standards to ensure accuracy, clarity, and reliability in every article we publish.</p><h3><strong>1. Evidence‑Based Information</strong></h3><p>All content is based on reputable medical sources, including:</p><ul><li><p>National Institutes of Health (NIH)</p></li><li><p>Mayo Clinic</p></li><li><p>Cleveland Clinic</p></li><li><p>PubMed and peer‑reviewed research</p></li><li><p>CDC and other established medical organizations</p></li></ul><p>We do not publish unverified claims, anecdotal cures, or speculative medical advice.</p><h3><strong>2. Clear, Accessible Writing</strong></h3><p>Our goal is to translate complex medical information into easy‑to‑understand guidance.
We avoid medical jargon whenever possible and prioritize clarity for seniors and caregivers.</p><h3><strong>3. Content Review Process</strong></h3><p>Every article is:</p><ul><li><p>Reviewed for accuracy</p></li><li><p>Checked against reputable medical sources</p></li><li><p>Updated when new research becomes available</p></li><li><p>Evaluated for clarity and readability</p></li></ul><h3><strong>4. No Medical Advice</strong></h3><p>Aging Health provides educational information only.
We do not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical care.</p><p>Readers are encouraged to consult qualified healthcare providers for personal medical decisions.</p><h3><strong>5. Transparency</strong></h3><p>We clearly label:</p><ul><li><p>Sources</p></li><li><p>Updates</p></li><li><p>Corrections</p></li><li><p>Sponsored content (if ever used)</p></li></ul><p>We do not accept payment for medical claims or product endorsements.</p><h3><strong>6. Updates &amp; Revisions</strong></h3><p>Health information changes.
We regularly update articles to reflect:</p><ul><li><p>New research</p></li><li><p>Updated guidelines</p></li><li><p>Improved clarity</p></li><li><p>Reader feedback</p></li></ul><h3><strong>7. Author Expertise</strong></h3><p>Content is written by Tommy Douglas, a health researcher and senior‑wellness advocate specializing in:</p><ul><li><p>Aging</p></li><li><p>Liver health</p></li><li><p>Dementia</p></li><li><p>Evidence‑based lifestyle strategies</p></li></ul><p>Our goal is to empower older adults and caregivers with trustworthy, easy‑to‑understand information.</p>
