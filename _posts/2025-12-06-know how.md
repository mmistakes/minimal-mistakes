** Steps to create "Buy me a Coffee** button in jekyll
---
1. Sign-up for Buy Me a Coffee using link https://buymeacoffee.com/brand
2. Above step should get you a **profile** link to integrate with minimal mistake theme
3. Use the profile link in _config.yml respective sections for display in your website
